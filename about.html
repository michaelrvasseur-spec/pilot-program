About us

A small studio, built for the long game.

Founded in Riverside in 2019. Locally owned, coach-run, and mission-driven — we exist to make serious training feel welcoming, measurable, and sustainable.

Our story

Opened in 2019 with one rack and one plan.

Marcus opened The Pilot Program after a decade coaching at big-box gyms that kept asking coaches to sell personal training instead of program it. He rented a 900-square-foot bay on Ironworks Ave, bought one rack, and started writing programs for friends.

Seven years later, we've grown into a 6,400-square-foot studio with three full-time coaches, thirty-eight weekly classes, and a member community that mostly found us through a friend. We still write every program ourselves. We still know every member's name.

Coaching philosophy

Three things we believe.

01

Progress is a program, not a vibe.

Good training has a structure — a mesocycle, a deload, an objective. If you can't see where you're headed, you won't stick around long enough to get there.

02

Coaches coach. Salespeople sell.

Our trainers don't earn commission on memberships or supplements. They earn a good wage for coaching well. It changes everything about how a session feels.

03

The best gym is the one you return to.

Adherence beats intensity nine days out of ten. We design programming, class caps, and the door policy around one question: will you come back Thursday?
