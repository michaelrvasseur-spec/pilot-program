Frequently asked

The honest answers.

Can I cancel my membership at any time?

Yes. Cancel from your member portal before the next billing date and you won't be charged again. No cancellation fees, no exit interviews, no phone trees.

Can I pause my membership?

You can pause a monthly membership for up to 60 days per calendar year — travel, injury, life. Annual plans can be paused for up to 90 days. Your billing pauses with it.

When am I billed?

Monthly memberships bill on the 1st of each month. Annual plans bill on the anniversary of your signup date. Every charge shows in your portal the moment it runs.

Do you offer family or household plans?

Yes — a second household member joins Core or Elite at 20% off. We verify with a shared address once, then nothing else changes. Ask a coach at the front desk.

What happens if I have to miss a prepaid personal-training session?

Cancel at least 12 hours ahead and the session returns to your pack. Same-day cancellations count as used, with one grace exception per quarter.

Do memberships auto-renew?

Monthly plans continue month-to-month until canceled. Annual plans auto-renew at the anniversary — we email a reminder 30 days out so you can opt out cleanly.
